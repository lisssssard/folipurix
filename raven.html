♟♛ The lying Queen ♛♟

☭ All ☭ ✁ Hasami ✁ ♡ Yukio ♡ ♱ Kaede ♱ ❀ Hitoku ❀ ♛ Raven ♛
Raven grew up in a very poor family, with her parents, her many siblings and her twin brother Lukas. She never felt like she was special to anyone or important in any way, making her yearn for attention and power. She started pretending like she was of noble blood and began to befriend other nobles and even the Crown prince. She started her friendships with lies and even got into a relationship with the prince to one day become queen. When she felt like it was too dangerous to keep living in her family she lied about them being kidnappers and let everyone of them except for her and Lukas be hanged publicly. While Lukas is against her decisions, he doesnt intervene since he knows it will make her happy.
